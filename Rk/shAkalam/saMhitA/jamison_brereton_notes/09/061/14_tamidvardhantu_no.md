The hapax tatpuruṣa hṛdaṃsáni- ‘gaining the heart’ is curious, in having an apparent acc. in -am to the neut. root noun hṛ́d-, as if to a masc. or fem. stem or to a thematic *hṛda- (see, e.g., AiG II.1.208).
