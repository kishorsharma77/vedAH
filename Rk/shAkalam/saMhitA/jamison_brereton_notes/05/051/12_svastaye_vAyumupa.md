Bṛhaspati’s “whole flock” (bv. sárva-gaṇa-) may refer to the Aṅgirases. In any case note sárva- rather than the older víśva-, esp. notable in a Viśve Devāḥ hymn (though víśve devā́ḥ opens the next vs.).
