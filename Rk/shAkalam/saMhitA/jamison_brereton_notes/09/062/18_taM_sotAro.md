This vs. is notable for its 2nd ps. address to the priests (sotāraḥ … hinota “o pressers, impel …”), also found in vss. 21, 29. Ordinarily 2nd ps. in soma hymns is reserved for Soma (sg.) and his drops (etc.) (pl.).
