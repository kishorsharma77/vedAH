Pādas a and b share a verb, duhate in b. Each pāda contains a contrastive pair: devébhyaḥ … náraḥ “the men for the gods” and sahásra(dhāram) … dáśa (kṣípaḥ) “thousand(-streamed) … ten (fingers).”
