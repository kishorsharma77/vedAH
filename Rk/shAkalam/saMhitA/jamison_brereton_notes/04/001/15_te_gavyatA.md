The hapax dṛdhrá- is plausibly explained by Hoffmann (reported in EWA s.v.) as a crossing of a redupl. nominal*dadhrá- (√dhṛ) and the ppl. dṛḍha (√dṛh), the latter found in the second hemistich (15c).
