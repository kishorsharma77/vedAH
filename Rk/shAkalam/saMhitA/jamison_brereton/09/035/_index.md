35 (747)
Soma Pavamāna
Prabhūvasu Āṅgirasa
6 verses: gāyatrī
Soma as the source and support of wealth and as ally in conflict is the theme of this  hymn, which is characterized by pleasing etymological figures (e.g., vss. 3, 4, 6) and  variant repetitions (e.g., vss. 2 + 5).
