The function of abhí, initial in c, is unclear; the verb of this hemistich, pṛṇadhvam, final in d, does not appear with abhí elsewhere.

On the triple-backed (tripṛṣṭhá-) soma, see Ge’s n. 1c, where he suggests among other possibilities that it refers to the three ingredients making up the soma drink (soma juice, water, and milk). 56
